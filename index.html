Our Story

Founded in 2010 in the heart of Badulla, ACM Spices began as a small family business with a passion for bringing the finest Sri Lankan spices to households across the island.

Our founder, Mr. Anura, comes from generations of spice farmers and traders. With deep roots in the Badulla region, we've built strong relationships with local farmers to source the freshest, highest quality spices while ensuring fair trade practices.

Today, ACM Spices has grown into a trusted name known for quality, authenticity, and customer service. We still maintain our small-business ethos while embracing modern practices to serve customers better.

Our Mission

To deliver authentic Sri Lankan spices while supporting local farmers and preserving traditional knowledge.
